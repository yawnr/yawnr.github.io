About

Oh, hey.

I guess I started coding when I was in high school on my TI-83 Plus calculator, when I wrote a short program to solve quadratic equations. Then, during my freshman year at Vassar, I took a few courses in Java. In my spare time, I built Flash games and websites, and continued to be intrigued with programming.

After a short detour in Finance (ahem, 6 years), I realized that the part of my job that I found to be the most interesting was writing VBA macros in Excel. And so I decided to become a full-time developer.

You can read my daily blog on my experience at App Academy here.
